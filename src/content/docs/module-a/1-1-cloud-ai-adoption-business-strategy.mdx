## What the Auditor Checks

The auditor verifies you have a **defined, data-driven process** that captures the cloud & AI business strategies used to guide customer decisions. The process must include, at minimum:

1. A strategy review that captures the customer's business needs and the problems they are trying to solve.
2. Personalized recommendations from the partner for the customer's business strategies.
3. Recommendations for maximizing cloud business value.

**Typical questions:**
- Walk me through the methodology you use to elicit and document the customer's business strategy.
- Show me the FinOps Review for these two customers.
- How did your recommendations differ between the two customers, and why?

| 5 | **Report, Presentation, or Documented Plan** capturing strategic inputs + decisions for both customers | PDF, PowerPoint | ⬜ |

> **FinOps Review is required for all partners and specializations.** Use either CASE or an Advisor Assessment as the second required assessment.

<Aside type="tip">
  Both projects must have been completed in the **past 12 months** and aligned to the 3-step process above. The plan must highlight **both Business and FinOps (Financial) outcomes** — not just one.
</Aside>

## Evidence Guidance

### FinOps Review
Run the [FinOps Review assessment](https://learn.microsoft.com/assessments/) for each customer; export the result. Capture the recommended actions in the customer's strategy plan.

### Cloud Adoption Strategy Evaluator (CASE)
Available in Microsoft Learn assessments. Document the customer's business motivations, expected outcomes, and business-case shape.

### Strategy plan
A 5–15 page document (or deck) per customer that combines the assessment outputs with the partner's personalised recommendations for both **business** and **financial** outcomes.

## Common Gaps

- **No FinOps Review on file:** It is **required for every partner and specialization**. Run it even if cost optimisation isn't the customer's primary driver.
- **Strategy decks that don't reference the assessments:** Cross-link CASE / FinOps outputs into the recommendations section explicitly.
- **Both customer projects are &gt; 12 months old:** Re-engage one of them on an extension or pick a more recent example.
- **Recommendations are generic:** Personalise to the customer's industry, footprint, and existing tooling.
