## Requirement 1 – Active Solutions Partner Designation

Your organisation must hold an active **Solutions Partner for Infrastructure (Azure)** designation.

| Designation | How to verify |
|---|---|
| Solutions Partner for **Infrastructure (Azure)** | Partner Center → Overview → Membership |

> ✅ **Evidence needed:** Screenshot of active designation from Partner Center, or the Partner Center PDF report showing designation status.

## Requirement 2 – Skilling Prerequisites

The Networking Services specialization audit retains skilling prerequisites (the 3rd-party certification requirement formerly in Module B Control 1.1 was **removed in V1.6**, but skilling prerequisites in Partner Center remain in force).

Recommended consultant-level certifications for the engagement team:

| Certification | Role |
|---|---|
| **AZ-700** Designing and Implementing Microsoft Azure Networking Solutions | Primary networking-architect credential |
| **AZ-104** Azure Administrator Associate | Operations / Day-2 |
| **SC-100** Cybersecurity Architect Expert | Secure edge / Firewall Premium / segmentation scenarios |
| **AZ-305** Azure Solutions Architect Expert | Cross-pillar architecture (supporting) |

Confirm the current Partner Center skilling prerequisites for your organisation on the day of application — Microsoft updates them periodically.

> ✅ **Evidence needed:** Microsoft Learn transcripts or certification verification links for each individual on the audit team.

## Requirement 3 – Customer Engagement Base (Module B)

Unless stated otherwise on a specific control, you will need:

| Need | Detail |
|---|---|
| **≥ 3 unique customers** | With networking-workload deployments completed in the **last 18 months** |
| **Completed projects only** | Solution went live in production AND has documented customer sign-off |
| **Native Azure networking services** | At least **5** of: VNet+Peering, ExpressRoute, VPN, Virtual WAN, CDN, Front Door, Load Balancer, Private Link, Traffic Manager, Application Gateway, Azure Firewall |
| **Workload scenarios covered** | At least one (1) of: secure network infrastructure, on-prem & branch connectivity, remote work at scale, secure global app delivery, 5G & edge computing |

> ✅ **Evidence needed:** Signed SOWs, solution design docs, architecture diagrams, HLDs / LLDs, as-built docs, project plans (any 2+ per control as specified).

## Requirement 4 – Customer Engagement Base (Module A)

For Module A controls you will need evidence from **two (2) unique customer projects** completed within the **last 12 months** demonstrating:

- A defined Cloud & AI Adoption Business Strategy supported by the **FinOps Review** (required) plus one other assessment (CASE recommended)
- A Cloud & AI Adoption Plan with cost-management report (pricing-calculator output) **and** DevOps capability assessment per customer
- A Security & Governance baseline using **Microsoft Defender for Cloud** (or 3rd-party) plus a **Cloud Adoption Security Review**
- A **Well-Architected Review** per workload (Go-Live or Standard) before deployment
- A repeatable **Azure Landing Zone** deployment via Bicep, Terraform, or ARM (ALZ Accelerator accepted), evidenced by an **ALZ Review** assessment
- A documented skilling plan
- Operations Management Tooling — Azure Monitor / Automation / Backup-Site-Recovery — plus one CI/CD security-evidence artifact (security scan, monitoring-dashboard export, audit-ready YAML pipeline, SBOM, or policy-as-code snapshot)

> ✅ **Module A reuse:** A previous Module A Pass from another Azure specialization audit (V2.0+) within the last 2 years waives Module A here. Expert MSP V1.9+ also satisfies Module A.

## Requirement 5 – Third-Party Remote Audit

Your organisation must **pass an ISSI-administered remote audit**.

<Steps>
1. Confirm pre-qualification in Partner Center
2. Submit application via Partner Center → **Azure** under the **Specialization** section
3. Microsoft validates eligibility and confirms to ISSI
4. ISSI schedules the audit within 2 business days; audit must be conducted within 30 calendar days
5. Provide live demonstrations, source documents, and SME personnel during the audit (4 hours for Module B, 8 hours for A+B)
6. Address any Open Action Items in the Gap Review Meeting (within 15 calendar days of receiving the Gap Report)
7. Receive Final Report within 5 business days; Microsoft updates Partner Center within 2 business days
</Steps>

> ✅ **Evidence needed:** This entire repository constitutes your evidence preparation package. Use [Module A](../module-a/1-1-cloud-ai-adoption-business-strategy/) and [Module B](../module-b/1-1-cloud-networking-requirements-assessment/) for control-level evidence.
